"Welcome to our Tic Tac Toe game!

A Tic Tac Toe game typically follows these basic rules:

Game Setup:

. The game is played on a 3x3 grid. . Two players take turns to play: one is 'X' and the other is 'O'.

Game Play:

. Players take turns placing their marker (X or O) in an empty cell on the grid. . The first player to align three of their markers horizontally, vertically, or diagonally wins the round. . If all cells are filled without any player achieving a three-in-a-row, the game is considered a draw.

Winning a Round:

. A player wins a round by having three of their markers in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

Multiple Rounds:

. If playing multiple rounds, keep track of the number of wins for each player. . After a predefined number of rounds (e.g., 3 rounds), the player with the most wins is declared the overall winner. . In case of a tie (equal number of wins), additional rounds can be played or the game can be declared a tie.

the Game:

. After completing the set number of rounds, the game board is reset to start a new series of rounds.
